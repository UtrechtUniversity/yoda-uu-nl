Yoda is a system for managing your research data. It offers you to the following functionality:

- [Storing data](Storing-data.html)
- [Collaborate with colleagues; add and remove users and their rights](User-management.html)
- [Add metadata to your data](Add-metadata.html)
- [Archive your data in a comprehensive data package](Archiving.html)
- [Publish your data (and get a DOI)](Publishing.html)
